# Phase 10 Context: Subtitles
**Created:** 2026-04-29
**Milestone:** v1.3 Media Enhancement
**Requirements:** MED-03, MED-04, MED-05

## Goal
Enable subtitle support for movies.

## Requirements
- MED-03: Admin can upload subtitle files (SRT/VTT)
- MED-04: Users can enable/disable subtitles during playback
- MED-05: Multiple subtitle tracks supported per movie
