# Milestone: v5.5 Mobile Experience

**Status:** Planning
**Phases:** 206-210

## Goal
Deliver a world-class mobile viewing experience with native apps, offline support, and mobile-optimized features.

## Phases

### Phase 206: Mobile Platform
**Goal:** Establish mobile platform foundation
**Requirements:** MP-01 to MP-05
**Focus:**
- Progressive Web App (PWA) with offline support and app-like experience
- Native iOS app with UIKit/SwiftUI and native video player
- Native Android app with Material Design and ExoPlayer integration
- Cross-platform responsive design system with mobile-first breakpoints
- Mobile-specific navigation patterns (bottom nav, gestures, swipe)

### Phase 207: Offline & Sync
**Goal:** Enable seamless offline viewing
**Requirements:** OS-01 to OS-05
**Focus:**
- Intelligent download manager with quality selection and Wi-Fi-only options
- Background download scheduling with network condition awareness
- Offline playback with DRM support and license caching
- Cross-device sync for watch progress, downloads, and preferences
- Smart content preloading based on viewing habits and travel patterns

### Phase 208: Mobile Playback
**Goal:** Optimize mobile video playback
**Requirements:** MB-01 to MB-05
**Focus:**
- Adaptive streaming with cellular data optimization and quality caps
- Picture-in-picture mode with background audio playback
- Mobile-optimized video controls (gesture seeking, brightness, volume)
- Battery-aware playback optimization with codec selection
- Data saver mode with audio-only option and reduced resolution

### Phase 209: Mobile Notifications
**Goal:** Engage users with smart notifications
**Requirements:** MN-01 to MN-05
**Focus:**
- Rich push notifications with inline playback and quick actions
- Smart notification scheduling with user timezone awareness
- Watch party and social activity notifications
- Content release and new episode alerts with personalization
- Download completion and storage warning notifications

### Phase 210: Mobile Integration
**Goal:** Deep platform integration
**Requirements:** MI-01 to MI-05
**Focus:**
- Deep linking with universal links and app clip support
- Biometric authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, fingerprint)
- Mobile payment integration (Apple Pay, Google Pay, carrier billing)
- Cast and AirPlay support with seamless device handoff
- Widget support for continue watching and recommendations
